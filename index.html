Welcome to Web Design by ELLIE X

Hey! My name is Lex and I am a design student with Eastern Washington University. My focus of design is in photographic media, and I'm currently learning to incorporate other forms of design into my photography, such as light design, fashion, and set production. I'm also learning how to expand my photography in post, such as color-grading and global edits, as well as expanding into print and digital photographic media publication. I'm really interested in the relationship between graphic and photographic arts! But for now, I am expanding my understanding of web design. Please enjoy my web design portfolio below!
